# Dynamic Automotive Solutions — Future Website Feature Plan

## Phase 1: Customer Experience Enhancements (Next 1–3 months)

### 1.1 Online Booking System
- Calendar-based appointment scheduling for services
- Service type selection with estimated duration and pricing
- Automated email/SMS confirmation and reminders
- Integration with workshop capacity management
- Reschedule and cancellation functionality

### 1.2 Customer Portal / Dashboard
- Secure login for returning customers
- View service history and past invoices
- Track current vehicle repair status in real-time
- Download receipts and service reports
- Save multiple vehicles to a single account

### 1.3 Live Chat / WhatsApp Integration
- WhatsApp Business API integration for instant communication
- Chatbot for FAQs (operating hours, pricing, location)
- Seamless handoff to a human agent when needed
- Chat history saved to customer profile

### 1.4 Push Notifications & Service Reminders
- Browser push notifications for service due dates
- Email reminders for upcoming scheduled maintenance
- Promotional notifications for seasonal offers

---

## Phase 2: Business Operations & Invoicing (3–6 months)

### 2.1 Invoicing & Payments System
- Generate professional invoices from the admin panel
- Email invoices directly to customers
- Online payment integration (Airtel Money, MTN Mobile Money, Visa/Mastercard)
- Payment status tracking (paid, pending, overdue)
- Recurring invoices for fleet maintenance contracts
- PDF invoice download and print functionality

### 2.2 Quotation System
- Create and send digital quotes to customers
- Customer can approve/reject quotes online
- Approved quotes auto-convert to work orders
- Quote expiry and follow-up reminders

### 2.3 Fleet Management Dashboard (for business clients)
- Dedicated portal for fleet managers
- Overview of all fleet vehicles and their service status
- Maintenance schedule calendar per vehicle
- Cost analytics and reporting (monthly/quarterly/annual)
- Bulk booking for multiple vehicles
- Export reports to CSV/PDF

### 2.4 Inventory & Parts Management
- Track spare parts stock levels
- Low-stock alerts and reorder notifications
- Parts cost tracking per job
- Supplier management

---

## Phase 3: Marketing & Growth (6–9 months)

### 3.1 Blog / Knowledge Centre
- SEO-optimised articles on vehicle maintenance tips
- Seasonal service guides (rainy season prep, long-trip checks)
- Video content embedding (YouTube tutorials)
- Category filtering and search
- Social media sharing integration

### 3.2 Customer Reviews & Testimonials
- Verified review system (post-service email prompts)
- Star ratings per service type
- Display reviews on service pages and homepage
- Google Reviews integration / widget

### 3.3 Referral Programme
- Unique referral codes for existing customers
- Discount or credit rewards for successful referrals
- Tracking dashboard for referral performance
- Automated reward notifications

### 3.4 Promotions & Offers Engine
- Create time-limited promotional offers
- Coupon code system for discounts
- Banner management for homepage promotions
- Email campaign integration for offer distribution

### 3.5 SEO & Analytics Enhancements
- Google Analytics 4 integration
- Conversion tracking for form submissions and bookings
- Heatmap integration (Hotjar/Microsoft Clarity)
- Automated sitemap updates
- Schema markup for reviews and FAQs

## Phase 4: Advanced Features (9–12 months)

### 4.1 Vehicle Diagnostics Report Portal
- Upload and share diagnostic scan reports with customers
- Visual health indicators (green/yellow/red) per system
- Recommendations linked to service booking
- Historical comparison of vehicle health over time

### 4.2 Multi-Language Support
- English (default) + Bemba, Nyanja, Tonga translations
- Language switcher in header
- Translated email templates

### 4.3 Mobile App (React Native)
- Native iOS and Android app
- Push notifications for service updates
- In-app booking and payment
- Digital loyalty card
- Vehicle document storage (insurance, fitness certificate)

### 4.4 Staff / Admin Dashboard
- Job card management system
- Technician assignment and workload tracking
- Time tracking per job
- Performance metrics and KPIs
- Role-based access control (admin, manager, technician)

### 4.5 Roadside Assistance & Recovery Tracking
- One-tap breakdown request from website/app
- GPS-based location sharing
- Real-time recovery vehicle tracking
- Estimated arrival time display
- Post-recovery service booking flow

## Phase 5: Enterprise & Integration (12+ months)

### 5.1 Accounting Software Integration
- QuickBooks / Xero / Sage integration
- Automatic sync of invoices and payments
- Financial reporting and tax summaries
- Multi-currency support (ZMW, USD)

### 5.2 CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
- Full customer lifecycle tracking
- Automated follow-ups and win-back campaigns
- Customer segmentation (fleet vs individual, service frequency)
- Sales pipeline for fleet contract negotiations

### 5.3 API for Third-Party Integrations
- RESTful API for partner integrations
- Webhook support for real-time event notifications
- Insurance company integration for claims processing
- Government COF (Certificate of Fitness) system integration

### 5.4 AI-Powered Features
- Predictive maintenance alerts based on vehicle history
- Smart pricing suggestions based on market data
- Automated customer support chatbot with AI
- Image-based damage assessment (upload photo → get estimate)

### 5.5 Loyalty & Rewards Programme
- Points-based system for repeat customers
- Tiered membership (Silver, Gold, Platinum)
- Birthday and anniversary rewards
- Partner discounts (fuel stations, car wash, insurance)
